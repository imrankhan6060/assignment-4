INTRODUCTION

PIGEON is a lifestyle brand that focuses on selling stylish and trendy products.

Inspired by international fashion trends, while mixing traditional culture with modern society, the Company’s creator registered the brand ‘PIGEON’ and its trademark in Korea in 2014. PIGEON is committed to selling good quality and affordable priced goods, as well as giving people the idea of “Easy Life,” spreading the tenacious, ever-lasting spirit of hibiscus across the globe.

Based on the “Let everyone enjoy life” brand mission, PIGEON has established its brand operation center in the world’s largest manufacturing country and the fashion capital of the world–Shanghai, China. Based on the strong retail sales context, the incorporation of global independent designers, high-quality supply chain tools, strong product development capabilities and productive logistics ecology, MUMUSO gradually made itself be known internationally after repeatedly persistent attempts.

In the future, PIGEON will continue to adhere to the principle of selling high-quality products, integrating global fashion with our product design and striving to create a complete industrial chain with design as its core, moving towards the big goal of where there are young people – there is PIGEON.

BRAND CONCEPT

PIGEON aims to make shopping and lifestyle easy simple and fun for young consumers through the mantra of Enjoy the Life. PIGEON always adheres to the Principle of selling products with reliable quality and affordable price, creating relaxing and lighthearted shopping experiences for consumers.
